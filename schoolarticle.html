CTU is a dynamic university with a wide range of programs to choose from, organized into four main areas of study:

COLLEGE OF ENGINEERING: For students interested in building, designing, and solving technical problems.
CTU-Danao's College of Engineering is a hub for aspiring engineers, offering a range of exciting programs to choose from. Students can explore fields like:
Bachelor of Science in Industrial Engineering (BSIE)
Bachelor of Science in Mechanical Engineering (BSME)
Bachelor of Science in Civil Engineering (BSCE)
Bachelor of Science in Electrical Engineering (BSEE)
Bachelor of Science in Computer Engineering (BSCpE)
With these diverse programs, CTU-Danao's College of Engineering is a great place to start your journey towards a rewarding career in engineering!

COLLEGE OF TECHNOLOGY: For those who love hands-on learning and practical skills in fields like automotive, computer, and electrical technology.
CTU-Danao's College of Technology is your gateway to a hands-on, practical education in exciting technical fields. They offer Bachelor of Industrial Technology (BIT) programs that focus on:
Bachelor of Industrial Technology (BIT) Major in Automotive Technology
Bachelor of Industrial Technology (BIT) Major in Computer Technology
Bachelor of Industrial Technology (BIT) Major in Drafting Technology
Bachelor of Industrial Technology (BIT) Major in Electrical Technology
Bachelor of Industrial Technology (BIT) Major in Electronics Technology
Bachelor of Industrial Technology (BIT) Major in Power Plant Technology
If you're interested in a career that combines technical skills with practical experience, the College of Technology at CTU-Danao is the place for you!

COLLEGE OF EDUCATION ARTS AND SCIENCES: For students passionate about teaching, exploring the arts, and gaining a strong foundation in the sciences.
CTU-Danao's College of Education is dedicated to nurturing future educators and equipping them with the skills to inspire young minds. They offer a variety of programs, including:
Bachelor of Elementary Education (BEEd)
Bachelor in Technology and Livelihood Education (BTLEd) major in Home Economics
Bachelor of Secondary Education (BSEd) Major in Mathematics
Bachelor of Secondary Education (BSEd) Major in Science
Bachelor of Secondary Education (BSEd) Major in Social Studies
Bachelor of Secondary Education (BSEd) major in Home Economics Technology (HET)
Diploma in Professional Education (DPE)
If you're passionate about education and want to make a difference in the lives of young people, CTU-Danao's College of Education is the perfect place to start your journey

COLLEGE OF MANAGEMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP: For aspiring business leaders and entrepreneurs who want to learn about managing organizations and starting their own ventures.
CTU-Danao's College of Hospitality and Tourism Management is your gateway to a world of exciting opportunities in the travel and hospitality industry. They offer programs that will equip you with the skills and knowledge to succeed in this dynamic field:
Bachelor of Science in Hospitality Management (BSHM)
Bachelor of Science in Tourism Management
If you're passionate about travel, hospitality, and creating unforgettable experiences, CTU-Danao's College of Hospitality and Tourism Management is the place to launch your career!

With these four colleges, CTU offers a diverse and exciting learning environment for students of all interests and aspirations.
